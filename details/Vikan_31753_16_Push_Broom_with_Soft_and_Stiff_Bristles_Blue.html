Vikan 31753 16" Push Broom

This durable push broom is built to deliver exceptional cleaning performance, making it a must-have for both home and commercial use. Its heavy-duty broom head stays firmly in place, providing consistent contact with the floor and reducing the need for frequent adjustments. The dual bristle design combines stiff bristles in the front for pushing large debris and soft bristles in the back to gather fine dust and particles, ensuring a thorough clean with every pass.

Angled bristles enhance sweeping efficiency, preventing the broom from hopping or skipping during use. The flared ends reach into tight corners and edges, making it easy to clean areas that are hard to access. Whether you're maintaining a garage, sidewalk, or high-traffic commercial space, this push broom is designed to save time and effort with its smart, versatile cleaning system.

The ergonomic Euro thread handle offers a secure and comfortable grip, enhancing control and minimizing strain during long cleaning sessions. This reliable tool is perfect for anyone looking to keep floors clean and well-maintained with minimal hassle, combining style and functionality for a seamless cleaning experience.
