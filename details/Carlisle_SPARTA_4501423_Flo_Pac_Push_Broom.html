Carlisle SPARTA 4501423 Flo-Pac Push Broom

The Carlisle SPARTA 4501423 Flo-Pac Push Broom is a powerful cleaning tool designed for both residential and commercial spaces. Made with moisture and chemical-resistant polypropylene bristles, this broom is built to handle spills and cleaning agents without losing its effectiveness or durability. Its 24" wide block size allows for quick cleaning of large areas, making it a time-saving solution for everyday messes.

The flagged bristles are perfect for smooth surfaces, lifting fine dust and debris with ease. The crimped design ensures that every sweep is efficient and effective, while the two threaded handle holes provide convenience and flexibility for alternating sweeping directions. This durable construction guarantees long-lasting use, helping you save on replacements and keep your floors spotless.

Perfect for Daily Cleaning

The 3" bristle trim prevents snagging on edges, ensuring smooth sweeping with no mess left behind. Its compact yet powerful design makes it a versatile addition to any cleaning routine.
